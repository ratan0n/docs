Text capabilities and use cases
Claude has a broad range of text-based capabilities, including but not limited to:
Capability
This enables you to…
Text Summarization
Distill lengthy content into key insights for executives, social media, or product teams.
Content Generation
Craft compelling content from blog posts and emails to marketing slogans and product descriptions.
Data / Entity Extraction
Uncover structured insights from unstructured text like reviews, news articles, or transcripts.
Question Answering
Build intelligent, interactive systems from customer support chatbots to educational AI tutors.

Seamlessly communicate across languages in products, support, and content creation.

Understand sentiment, preferences, and patterns to personalize user experiences and offerings.

Create engaging, context-aware interactions in games, virtual assistants, and storytelling apps.
